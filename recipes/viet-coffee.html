Vietnamese Coffee

Cà Phê Sữa Đá- Vietnamese Iced Coffee with condensed milk

    Ingredients

  • 4 tsp Trung Nguyen coffee
  • 2 tsp condensed milk
  • 1 cup boiling water

      • Steps

  • Boil the water
  • Preheat the filter and cup by pouring a bit of boiling water through it.
  • Remove the metal filter and pour in 1 heaping tablespoon of Trung Nguyen coffee
  • Twist the filter on gently until it just starts to stop. Then turn it little more, a bit less than 1/8 a turn.
  • Pour a tiny bit of water in the filter just to wet the grind and to let the grind expand a bit.
  • Fill the filter all the way and let it drip. Ideal brewing time comes to about 3 to 5 minutes so adjust the filter accordingly.
  • Add the condensed milk
  • Let it cool off a bit then pour the brew into a glass filled with ice.
